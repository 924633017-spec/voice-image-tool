# Gallery Noir Redesign

Date: 2026-06-19

## Direction

Reposition the product as a premium storytelling surface instead of a polished SaaS utility.

Core aesthetic:

- Front stage: dark exhibition / digital gallery
- Playback: cinematic artwork viewer
- Dashboard: curated creator desk
- Editor: director console

## Surface Goals

### Homepage

- Replace light SaaS framing with a dark hero stage
- Lead with artwork, not feature cards
- Use poster-like typography and sparse, high-contrast copy
- Make hotspot storytelling the unforgettable visual idea

### Playback

- Treat the page as a presentation surface for a finished work
- Use a dark stage, restrained hotspot styling, and larger subtitle treatment
- Make the active narration feel like a guided exhibition moment

### Dashboard

- Shift from project management to curation
- Highlight a featured work first
- Make drafts and playable works feel like a catalog, not a file grid

### Editor

- Keep the image dominant
- Reduce toolbar weight
- Focus the right panel on the currently selected hotspot as a narration card
- Make recording feel like directing a scene, not filling a form

## Visual Rules

- Near-black backgrounds with warm undertones
- High-contrast serif display typography
- Refined accent red, used sparingly
- Thin borders, glow restraint, gallery-like spacing
- Motion should feel atmospheric rather than playful
